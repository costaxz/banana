Spider-Man

  • Alter Ego: Peter Parker
  • Powers: Superhuman strength, speed, and agility; Genius-level intellect; Web shooters; "Spidey-sense"
  • Enemies: Green Goblin, Doctor Octopus, Venom, Kingpin, J. Jonah Jameson
  • Allies: Mary Jane Watson, Aunt May Parker, Harry Osborn, Gwen Stacy, Felicia Hardy, Uncle Ben Parker
  • "With great power comes great responsibility."
